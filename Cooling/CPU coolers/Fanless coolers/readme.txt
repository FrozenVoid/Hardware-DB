Fanless coolers circulate various liquids
in their pipes which transfer heat from heatsink
interface touching the CPU: while they don't approach
the performance of mainstream air coolers they emit zero
noise.
The best fanless cooler is currently
made by NoFan(NoFan CR-95C).
